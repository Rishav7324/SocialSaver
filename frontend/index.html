About SocialSaver

SocialSaver is a free, open-source online downloader that lets you save videos, audio, and playlists from virtually any social media platform. Built on yt-dlp and FastAPI, it supports over 1000 websites including YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Twitter/X, Facebook, Reddit, Vimeo, SoundCloud, Twitch, Dailymotion, and many more.

There are no ads, no sign-up required, no daily limits, and no hidden fees. The project is fully open source under the MIT license — contributions are welcome!

Frequently asked questions

Is SocialSaver free?

Yes — 100% free. No sign-up, no subscriptions, no hidden fees. SocialSaver is also open source under the MIT license.

What platforms do you support?

1000+ platforms including YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Twitter/X, Facebook, Reddit, Vimeo, SoundCloud, Twitch, Dailymotion, and many more. Powered by yt-dlp.

How do I download a video?

Copy the URL of the video, paste it into the input field above, click Get Info, choose your preferred format, and click Download. It's that simple!

What formats are available?

You can download as MP4 video (various resolutions up to 4K), MP3 audio, WebM, and more depending on what the source platform offers.

Can I download entire playlists?

Yes! Paste a playlist or channel URL and SocialSaver will list all videos. You can download individual videos or the entire playlist.

Is there a limit on downloads?

There is no hard limit, but rate limiting is in place to ensure fair usage for everyone (60 requests per minute).

Is my privacy protected?

We do not log or store your downloaded URLs, and no personal data is collected. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I contribute to the project?

Absolutely! SocialSaver is open source on GitHub. Feel free to submit issues, feature requests, or pull requests.

Open Source

SocialSaver is released under the MIT license. The entire source code is available on GitHub. Contributions, bug reports, and feature requests are always welcome.
