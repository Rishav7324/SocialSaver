Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. This policy describes what information we collect and how it is handled.

1. Information We Collect

URLs: The URLs you submit are processed to fetch media information. These are not permanently stored.

Log Data: Our servers may log anonymous request data including timestamps and basic request metadata for operational purposes.

Local Storage: If you use the download history feature, data is stored locally in your browser. We do not access this data.

Theme Preference: Your chosen color theme (dark/light) is stored locally in your browser.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the Service
  • To improve and optimize the Service
  • To monitor usage patterns and prevent abuse

3. Data Retention

Downloaded files are stored temporarily on our servers and are automatically deleted after 30 minutes. We do not keep permanent copies of any media.

4. Third-Party Services

The Service uses yt-dlp (an open-source tool) to process media from third-party platforms. These platforms have their own privacy policies.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

We do not use tracking cookies. The only local storage used is for your download history and theme preference, which never leaves your browser. No personal data is transmitted to third parties.

6. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with anyone. Your privacy is our priority.
